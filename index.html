A couple of facts about chocolate.

  • White chocolate isn't technically chocolate because it does not contain cocoa.
  • The cocoa tree is native to the Equatorial South America
  • Types of chocolates: milk, "white," and dark chocolate.
  • Three main stages to chocolate production are fermentation, drying, and roasting.
  • The worlds largest chocolate weighted 5,792.50 kg (Source: Guinness World Records).

The Chocolate Making Process:
  1. Harvesting
  2. Fermentation
  3. Drying
  4. Roasting
  5. Winnowing
  6. Grinding
  7. Conching
